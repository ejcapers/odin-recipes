Salmon

Description

This salmon recipe is quick, beginner friendly, and delicious. This particular recipe serves four people, so you can show off your baking skills to your friends and family. This is a personal favorite of mine as it is the first meal I made which I was truly proud of. From the satisfying texture of flaky salmon to the lemony garlic sauce, if you follow all the steps, you too can make a meal you are proud of. The end result will be a perfectly baked salmon paired wonderfully with a lemon garnish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lemon
  • Butter
  • Minced Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Italian herb Seasoning Blend

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees and grease a large baking pan. Arrange the salmon filets on the baking pan and seasoonl generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Stir together the olive oil, garlic, herbs, and juice of 1/2 lemon. Spoon this sauce over your fillets, making sure the tops and sides are covered; there should be no dry spots. Thinly slice the remaining 1/2 lemon and top each piece of salmon.
  3. Bake the salmon in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is opaque and flaky when pulled apart with a fork.
  4. Garnish with fresh thyme or parsley if desired and serve.
