Chicken and Waffles Casserole

Description

This recipe is a blend of two yummy foods all in one. Buckle up, because this recipe is on the longer side in terms of duration of other recipes on this site. However, we promise it is well worth the wait! This is easy to put together and has sweet and savory compnents. It is a phenomenal way to change up the brucnch casserole and makes for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 frozen Belgian Waffles
  • 1 1/2 pounds frozen chicken nuggets, slightly thawed
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 1/3 cups half-and-half
  • 2 tablesppons maple syrup, plus more for serving
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange waffles on a baking sheet.
  2. Bake waffles in the oven until lightly toasted on each side, about 4 minutes per side. Remove; cut each waffle into 8 pieces, and arrange waffles in the prepared baking dish.
  3. Cut larger chicken nuggets in half, leaving smaller pieces whole, and arrange among waffles.
  4. Whisk together eggs, half-and-half, 2 tablespoons maple syrup, salt, adn cayenne pepper in a large bowl until well combined and slightly frothy.Pour egg mixture over waffles and chicken; cover and let stand 15 minutes to soak before baking.
  5. Bake until eggs are set and top is golden brown, about 1 hour, uncovering halfway through baking time. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before serving. Drizzle with melted butter and syrup.
