Pasta with Olive Oil and Garlic

Description

Everybody loves a good pasta recipe. This meal is perfect for any week night as it is simple to make on top of being delicious. This recipe is also incredibly flexible. Following the steps as written will leave you satisfied, but feel free to add your own garnishes along the way and really get creative with it. For insatnce, feel free to add chicken of beef along the way, or garnish with your particular prefernce of herbs. The possibilities truly are endless!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rigatoni or other tubular pasta
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 3 medium garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley

Directions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water, then add the pasta and cook until al dente, about 12 minutes. Drain and return pasta to pot.
  2. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, combine the oil, butter, garlic, salt, and a few grinds of pepper. Cool over medium high heat, stirring often, until the garlic is softened, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Toss the hot pasta with the olive oil sauce and parsley and serve.
